Science as a way of seeing the world.

I'm passionate about unraveling how the physical world works — and translating that understanding into tools for a more electrified future. My work sits at the intersection of electrochemistry, reaction engineering, and the complex beauty of how things move and react.

Beyond the lab, I communicate science through articles, comics, and photography. Curiosity is the throughline.
